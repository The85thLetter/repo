Reznat

Reznat is radical and a passionate gamer, he is known for being an expert in RPGs and puzzle games like sudoku. Reznat does not have a large variety of games which is why he is so good at the games he has played during his life. He won his hat at a gaming convention for participation. Friends of Reznat have complained that he never leaves anything for anyone and keeps everything to himself, if he wins a prize he keeps it. The only time he lets go of something he gained, it has to be a bad quality item.

Essoneg

Essoneg is known for drawing sword art created online and for the town they live in. Art helps them escape their problems. Essoneg is very troubled and always has stress on their hands. When Essoneg draws, their mind is clear and they imagine the world in an entire different way. They are easily amused by anything related to art. Their favorite drawing is a sword they drew when they were sitting at a cafe across from an antique store. Essoneg loves history, especially battles in which there were swords. Others stay away from Essoneg because of how they always draws battles and decapitations. Authorities did not like Essoneg so they isolated them.

Enolem Enies

Nereitkepser

Nereitkepser are shaped like some hat types and they can sneak their ways on to people’s wardrobe and get on their heads. Once they get on someone’s head they gather information and give it to their holder’s enemy. They are used by large groups to gather information on enemy forces.
